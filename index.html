FAQ

Everything you need to know

Galaxy London enforces a no-alcohol policy for guests under 18 and requires valid photo ID upon arrival. Reach out for bespoke events and multilingual support.

Do you cater to large parties?

Absolutely. From intimate 6-person suites to 20+ guest takeovers, our team curates menus, AV, and bottle service to match your vibe.

What are the service hours?

We operate late into the night with extended hours on weekends. Contact us for peak availability and after-hours access.

Can I bring my own cake?

Yes. Let our team know ahead of time and we’ll prepare plating and storage so your celebration flows seamlessly.

Do you have non-alcoholic options?

Our bar programme includes zero-proof cocktails, premium teas, and fresh juices to keep every guest in the mix.
